Polynomials are mathematical expressions with variables raised to non-negative integer powers.
They take the form of anxn+an-1xn-1+⋯+a1x+a0 where an,an-1,⋯,a1,a0 are coefficients.
The highest power of x is the degree of the polynomial.
Polynomials are essential in algebra and can be used to represent various mathematical relationships.
